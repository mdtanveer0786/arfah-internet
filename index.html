Frequently Asked Questions

How long does Aadhar correction take?

Typically 7-10 working days after submission. We provide tracking facility to monitor your application status.

What documents do I need for PAN card application?

You need proof of identity (Aadhar, Passport, Driving License), proof of address, and passport size photographs.

Do you provide home service?

Yes, we provide home service for document collection and delivery with an additional nominal charge of ₹100.

What are your working hours?

We are open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Is my personal information safe with you?

Absolutely! We follow strict confidentiality protocols and your documents are handled with utmost security.

What is the process for opening a bank account?

We help you with complete bank account opening process including document preparation, form filling, and bank coordination. You need Aadhar, PAN card, address proof, and passport photos.

How much time does passport application take?

Normal passport application takes 15-30 days after document submission. Tatkal passport can be processed in 7-10 days with additional charges.

Do you provide refund if service is not completed?

Yes, we offer 100% refund if we fail to complete the service due to our fault. However, government fees are non-refundable once paid.

Can I get same-day service for urgent work?

Yes, we offer express service for urgent requirements with 50% additional charges. Please call us in advance for same-day service availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards, and net banking. 50% advance is required for all services.

How do I track my application status?

We provide application tracking number and regular updates via WhatsApp. You can also visit our center or call us for status updates.

Do you provide services for students?

Yes, we offer special discounts for students on services like exam form filling, scholarship applications, resume making, and educational document attestation.

What is the cost for resume making service?

Basic resume starts at ₹50, professional resume with cover letter costs ₹100, and premium resume with LinkedIn optimization costs ₹200.

Do you help with government scholarship forms?

Yes, we specialize in government scholarship applications including OBC, SC/ST, minority scholarships, and merit-based scholarships with documentation support.

Can you help with property registration documents?

Yes, we provide complete assistance with property registration, stamp duty payment, mutation certificate, and land tax payment services.

Do you provide courier service for documents?

Yes, we offer document courier service through reputed courier companies. Charges vary based on location and document urgency.
